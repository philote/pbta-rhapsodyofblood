The Packrat is comprehensively prepared for this expedition. Within their pack you might find the perfect weapon, the needed medicine, or just the matches you need to light your pipe.
Play the Packrat if you want to be a resourceful support character, command minions, and play around with explosives.

Instructions: You can drag-and-drop into your character sheet any Item you choose and it will automatically be assigned to its correct place. Everything else needs to be copy-pasted or input manually (this includes the background & details and your stats).
