The Slayer is a monster hunter, plain and simple. They’re fast and lethal, equally at home confronting a horde of beasts and a single towering colossus.
Play the Slayer if you want to be right in the centre of every melee, flinging yourself into combat safe in the knowledge it'll hurt them more than it'll hurt you.

Instructions: You can drag-and-drop into your character sheet any Item you choose and it will automatically be assigned to its correct place. Everything else needs to be copy-pasted or input manually (this includes the background & details and your stats).
